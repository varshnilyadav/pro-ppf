02 — The Technology

The PPF
Advantage

Next-generation thermoplastic urethane engineered to be
the last line of defense your paint ever needs.

Self-Healing

Minor scratches and swirl marks vanish with heat exposure, keeping your finish perpetually pristine.

Technology

Advanced thermoplastic urethane polymers realign at the molecular level when exposed to heat — from sunlight or warm water — erasing light scratches within minutes. Your vehicle's paint maintains its showroom luster indefinitely.

01

Flawless Finish

Ultra-clear, optically transparent film preserves the original color depth and gloss of your paint.

Clarity

Our premium-grade PPF boasts 99.7% optical clarity with zero orange peel effect. The film is virtually invisible on the surface, maintaining your vehicle's factory paint aesthetics while adding an additional layer of deep, wet-look gloss.

02

Impact Resistance

200-micron thick film absorbs rock chips, road debris, and insect acids before they reach your paint.

Defense

Multi-layered construction with an elastomeric polymer core disperses impact energy across the film surface, preventing stone chips, sand abrasion, and chemical contaminants from ever making contact with your vehicle's clear coat.

03

UV Protection

Blocks harmful UV radiation to prevent paint oxidation, fading, and yellowing over time.

Longevity

Built-in UV inhibitors within the film's top coat filter out 99% of harmful ultraviolet radiation. This prevents the molecular breakdown of your vehicle's clear coat, keeping colors vibrant and preventing the chalky oxidation that plagues unprotected vehicles.
